About AIC

AIC was formed by Nelson Mandella in 1997 to facilitate the development of Industrial consultants and was originally wholly owned by the Atlantic Industrial Consultant. AIC activities focused on expanding it's wide range of skills for business which includes problem solving ability and overall guidance for their valuable clients. Ma'aden has also developed its activities beyond gold with the development of Ma'aden Phosphate Company, which started production in 2011, its aluminium project which is currently under construction and a number of other projects. Ma'aden's exploration teams are working to expand available resources in existing business areas as well as to broaden the company's mineral portfolio.
